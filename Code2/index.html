FAQ's

+ What is EDVORA and who can use it?

EDVORA is a platform designed to connect students with alumni for mentorship, guidance, networking, and opportunities. It can be used by students who are seeking support in interviews, exams, projects, and careers, as well as by alumni who want to share their knowledge, offer mentorship, and create opportunities for the student community.

+ What type of resources are available on EDVORA?

EDVORA provides access to a variety of resources shared by alumni, including study materials, project ideas, interview preparation guides, career advice, and networking opportunities.
